Health Care Solutions


Our expertise in health care software development allows us to address the unique challenges faced by health care providers, hospitals, and regulatory agencies. We offer a wide range of solutions tailored to the health care industry, including:

  • 01License Management

    Our software solutions provide a comprehensive platform for efficient management of licenses for health care professionals, facilities, and institutions. We automate the entire license lifecycle, from application and review to renewal processes. By ensuring compliance with regulatory standards, reducing administrative burdens, and providing real-time visibility into license status, our solution empowers organizations to maintain regulatory compliance effortlessly.

  • 02Inspection and Regulation:

    Our software streamlines the inspection and regulation processes for health care facilities. We develop robust systems that enable seamless scheduling, conduct of inspections, generation of detailed reports, and tracking of compliance with regulations and quality standards. The solution improves resource allocation, reduces manual effort, and ensures adherence to regulatory requirements, enhancing overall operational efficiency.

  • 03Electronic Health Records (EHR):

    We specialize in developing secure and user-friendly electronic health record systems that enable health care providers to efficiently maintain and access patient data. Our EHR solutions focus on data integrity, privacy, and interoperability, empowering health care professionals with comprehensive patient information for informed decision-making. By facilitating seamless data exchange, improving patient care coordination, and enhancing overall operational efficiency, our EHR solutions drive improved health outcomes and patient satisfaction.

 

Food and Medicine Regulatory Solutions


HaPo excels in providing software solutions for food and medicine regulatory agencies, ensuring adherence to safety standards, quality control, and efficient operations. Our expertise includes the following areas:

  • 01Form Processing

    Our software solutions streamline the processing and management of regulatory forms related to food and medicine. Leveraging intelligent automation, we develop solutions that extract relevant data, validate information, and integrate it with relevant systems. By reducing manual effort, improving accuracy, and expediting processing times, our form processing solution enhances data quality, regulatory compliance, and decision-making.

  • 02Business Process Management

    Our solutions enable seamless management of regulatory processes, from product registration to safety evaluations. We develop customized workflows that automate approval processes, standardize operations, and provide real-time visibility into regulatory operations. By automating complex processes, enhancing efficiency, ensuring transparency, and enabling regulatory compliance, our business process management solution empowers regulatory agencies to effectively monitor and enforce regulations.

Project and Government Engagements


These projects demonstrate our ability to deliver sophisticated software solutions that meet the diverse needs of government agencies and organizations in the health care, food, and medicine regulatory domains.

  • 01HealthCarePro

    We developed a comprehensive health care management system for a government health department. The solution integrated license management, inspection processes, and electronic health records into a single platform. It automated workflows, enabled real-time data exchange, and provided advanced analytics for better decision-making. The HealthCarePro system significantly improved efficiency, reduced paperwork, and enhanced patient care.

  • 02ReguFood

    We developed ReguFood, a regulatory software solution designed for a national food safety authority. The system streamlined the registration process for food establishments, automated inspection and enforcement activities, and facilitated real-time data analysis for risk assessment. ReguFood improved compliance, reduced turnaround times, and enhanced the overall safety and quality of the food supply chain.

  • 03MediTrack

    MediTrack is a medicine regulatory system developed for a pharmaceutical regulatory agency. The solution provided end-to-end tracking of medicine registration, streamlined compliance processes, and offered analytical tools for drug safety monitoring. By automating complex regulatory workflows, MediTrack improved regulatory efficiency, ensured timely evaluations, and enhanced patient safety.
